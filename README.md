

# UtopiaHire – AI-Powered Career Guidance

**Design & Methodology Report – IEEE CS & CN & CyberSecurity Challenge**

---

## Overview

UtopiaHire is an AI-powered platform aimed at promoting fairness and inclusivity in employment, targeting job seekers in Sub-Saharan Africa and the MENA region. The platform helps candidates enhance their resumes, prepare for interviews, and receive personalized career development recommendations using ethical and contextualized AI.

The current prototype includes functional modules for:
- **CV Checker** – analyzes resumes, extracts information, provides structured feedback, and estimates ATS compatibility.
- **Video Interview Module** – simulates AI-driven interviews, analyzes facial expressions and vocal patterns, and provides inclusive feedback.
- **Personal Development / Recommendations Module** – suggests online courses, certifications, and skill-building resources.

Other modules are planned but not yet functional due to time constraints.

---

## Features

- **User Account Management**: Signup, login, and profile management.
- **CV Analysis**: NLP-based resume checking and feedback.
- **Video Interview Simulation**: AI-driven interviews with stress and voice analysis.
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Career growth, certifications, and soft skills improvement.
- **Inclusivity**: Analysis considers users with speech or pronunciation difficulties to ensure fair assessment.

---

## Technical Details

- **Front-End**: User interface for login, signup, and module interaction.

- **APIs & Libraries**:
  - [DeepFace](https://github.com/serengil/deepface) – facial analysis
  - [Librosa](https://librosa.org/) – audio processing

  - [Hugging Face Transformers](https://huggingface.co/transformers/) – language models
